Services


What we offer

Pack

At GPM, we offer fully customizable packing services, we can pack just your utensils and fragile items or your entire home. This service is used by most of our clients and is great option for those who don’t have time for it all. We care about your household items as our own, That's how we stand out from peers.

Move

We offer short and long distance moving and take pride ourselves on timeliness, speed and efficiency as well as commanding a friendly and courteous crew to attend to all of our clients’ needs. Our goal is to take all the stress and anxiety out of relocation and to make our clients feel as comfortable as possible.

Labor Only

Sometimes you need to rearrange things around the house or relocate to same building. This is exactly what our LABOR ONLY service is designed for. Without the need for a truck, we can provide the same quality of service at a reduced rate and get even the simplest of moves done the right way.

Vehicle Shifting

Planning to relocate your vehicle, but worried about transportation? Well, worry not because GPM ships your automobiles efficiently and in a good time with door to door transportation. We at our core of heart believe to enrich your experience by relieving you from all concerns regarding shifting your vehicles. Just tell us your vehicle type, locations, date and leave rest to us.

Hire Truck

At GPM, we're proud to offer a large and versatile fleet to hire trucks. Whether you're looking to hire a wagon for home or office relocations, or you're planning a road trip with lots of gear, we have the best vehicle for you. Our top-quality fleet ranges from trailers to all available size of transportation trucks. We're happy to help with advice on the best hire vehicle for your needs. .

Storage

Need a safe place to store your goods? Well, your concerns now can come to an end as GPM now provides an exclusive service to store your goods. We offer short and long-term storage options in our warehouses which can accommodate a variety of storage items.
